# Inside the DGX Spark — GB10, 128 GB Unified, 1 PFLOP on Your Desk

A low-level look at NVIDIA's personal AI workstation. The GB10 superchip pairs a 20-core ARM CPU (10 Cortex-X925 + 10 Cortex-A725) with a Blackwell GPU through in-package NVLink-C2C, exposes 128 GB of unified LPDDR5x at 273 GB/s, and delivers ~1 PFLOP of sparse FP4 in a 170 W desktop box. Block diagram, board layout, ports, cooling, and the silicon decisions that define what the box can do. Includes an interactive capability calculator.
